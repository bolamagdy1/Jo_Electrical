VFD’S

Controlling the speed of a motor is essential in today's manufacturing world. Variable Frequency Drives (VFD's) make this possible and save energy. At The Three Phase Co. we recognize that down time in your production driven environment is not acceptable. Customers who have used our configuration backup service experienced a significant reduction in down time when there placement of a VFD was required. Our technicians have the knowledge and expertise to make programming changes, perform complete replacements, upgrade and interface your VFD with PLC's and HMI's to fully automate your system.
